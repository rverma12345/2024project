Tips for College Costs

  • Consider living off-campus to save on housing costs.
  • Look for scholarships and grants to reduce tuition fees.
  • Calculate the total cost of commuting versus living on campus.
  • Factor in hidden costs like books and supplies.
  • Utilize student discounts on transportation and groceries.
  • Create a budget to track expenses and stick to it.
  • Consider community college for general education courses before transferring.
